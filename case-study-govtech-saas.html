GovTech SaaS
Outbound

The Problem

GovTech is a hyper-narrow market. The total addressable market is small. Bad data does not just cause a bounce. It burns a bridge with a prospect you cannot afford to lose. In this space, safety matters more than volume.

The Build

This is a signal-based outbound system built for precision. I ran a pilot on 50 highly targeted companies.

  • Enrichment: Scored and verified contact data across multiple sources.
  • Logic: Built dynamic email logic to personalize based on verified firmographics.
  • Safety Gate: Everything is gated. The system is structurally blocked from shipping if a critical data field is missing or unverified.
The Principle

When the market is small, you protect the list. Gated outbound means you never burn a high-value target on bad data.

The Metrics

What this is built to move:

  • Bounce rate: Driven to near zero by strict enrichment gating.
  • Personalization accuracy: Every email matches the company's specific GovTech profile.
  • Pipeline generated: High-value meetings secured without burning the TAM.
